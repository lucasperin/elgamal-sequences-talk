    We run experiments over two distinct data sets of pairs $(p, v)$ with $p > 1,000,000$ and $2 \leq v \leq 8$.

        \item[all primes:] Primes where $v \mid p -1$.
        \item[$g = v$ primes:] Primes where $v \mid p -1$ and $v$ is a generator.
